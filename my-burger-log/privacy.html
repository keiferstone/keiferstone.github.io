Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 18, 2025

My Burger Log ("the App") is a personal food journal designed to help users log and track the burgers they try. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data

The App does not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII). You are not required to create an account or log in to use the App.

User-Provided Content

The App allows you to optionally input and store information such as:

  • Burger names
  • Restaurant names
  • Ratings, notes, and toppings
  • Photos you choose to add

This content is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted or uploaded anywhere.

2. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Storage / Photo Access — to allow you to attach photos to burger entries
  • Location (optional) — to tag burger entries with general location (city/state)

These permissions are optional and only used to support app functionality. No location data is collected or transmitted by the developer.

3. Advertising

The App may use third-party services such as Google AdMob to display ads. These services may collect anonymous usage data as described in their own privacy policies.

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to remove ads and unlock additional features. All purchases are processed securely through the Google Play billing system. No personal payment data is handled or stored by the developer.

5. Data Retention

All data you enter is stored only on your device. If you uninstall the App, your data will be permanently deleted unless you've backed it up.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be posted within the App or through the Play Store listing.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer at:

Email: appsbykeif@gmail.com

My Burger Log respects your privacy and is committed to keeping your data local, private, and in your control.
